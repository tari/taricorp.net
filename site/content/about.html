Generally

This is taricorp.net, dumping grounds of the ideas, thoughts, and random musings of myself, Peter Marheine. The navigation links to the right list the site's main categories: - **Home** - the main page, with listings of all the latest activity. - **About** - this page! - **Blog** - the blog, where little things and general pointlessness which don't belong in any other category go. - **Projects** - repository of various projects of mine, particularly hardware and software development. - **Muse** - the place for more meaningful articles and thoughts- as in the blog, but more complete. - **Contact** - a way to contact me, should you have no other way to do so.

This site

The site is served from a machine instance running [Arch Linux](http://www.archlinux.org/) in Amazon's [EC2](http://aws.amazon.com/ec2/) cloud service, with with [lighttpd](http://www.lighttpd.net/) handling the actual serving duties. Content pages are generated by [Hyde](http://ringce.com/hyde), a Python static site generator with a few site processors and template tags of my own creation. All templates and pages are hand-written, mostly in [Notepad++](http://notepad-plus-plus.org/) and [vim](http://www.vim.org/), so you're likely to find some little bonuses in the page source, should the desire strike.

Accessibility

I strive to ensure that everything on the site is handled gracefully even in less-capable user agents such as [elinks](http://www.elinks.cz/) or [w3m](http://w3m.sourceforge.net), most significantly by avoiding the use of client-side scripting (i.e., JavaScript) wherever possible, but also by including images only sparingly. The site is also available over ipv6 at [ipv6.taricorp.net](http://ipv6.taricorp.net) for everyone out there who wants to be future-proof. {% endmarkdown %} {% endblock %}
